Your Future with Us

Begin Your Journey With Us

Becoming a real estate agent is one of the most obvious benefits of a real estate license. The majority of people get their licenses because they want to help people buy and sell real estate. Another license perk is the ability to shift careers if you don’t enjoy being a real estate agent. There are 3 jobs you are eligible for with a real estate license. Those jobs are a commercial agent, a leasing agent, and a property manager.

First Galaxy Inc. is a company for the here and now as well as the future. For friends, for family. For our fast-growing and connected community and every individual within it. Begin your real estate journey today. Begin your journey with us.
